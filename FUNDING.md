## How Karta is funded

Karta is founded and operationally funded by **CloudDon**
(clouddon.ai), an AI research platform, whose founder
Sriram serves as Karta's founding human operator.

CloudDon's sponsorship covers:
1. API costs (the primary operational expense)
2. Infrastructure costs (GitHub Actions minutes, Vercel, domain)
3. Reserve for unexpected cost spikes

**Sponsorship does not confer governance rights.**
CloudDon cannot direct the roadmap, request features,
influence agent behavior, or accelerate issue resolution.
Karta-0 makes all project decisions. Full stop.

Karta-0 publishes a monthly cost breakdown in OPERATIONS.md.

If you find the project valuable commercially, sponsorship is welcome
but entirely voluntary.
